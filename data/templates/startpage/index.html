About Us

This project is a "Jugend-Forscht" project from Mecklenburg Vorpommern. It is dedicated to the conception and programming of a database for medical explants.

Our Team

Our team consists of 2 people so far. In addition, we receive support from Dr. Daniel Klüß from the University of Rostock, who is available to answer our questions and is involved in the project.

Development

For the development we rely on the frontend framework Bootstrap in version v5.2 and in the backend the web framework Django.

Support

We would be very pleased about their cooperation in our project. So if you have any comments or encounter a problem, feel free to contact us.

Starting point

In order to better understand implant damage and make possible improvements in manufacturing or implantation, it is important to collect and evaluate data on damage. However, this data is often confusing and difficult to access because it is stored in different medical facilities and in different formats.

Project

A suitable and modern method of processing data is to enter this data into a database, as this allows access for all parties involved. The goal of the research is to develop a functional database capable of storing and, if necessary, evaluating large amounts of data on explant damage.
